Detailed Description

template<bool Parallel_Edges, typename T>
class network_butcher::types::WGraph< Parallel_Edges, CNode< Content< T > >, Time_Type >

Just another weighted graph class... Specialized for Template_Node_Type=CNode<Content<T>> and T_Weight_Type=Time_Type

Template Parameters
Parallel_EdgesIf true, the graph will support parallel edges
TThe type of the content of the nodes
